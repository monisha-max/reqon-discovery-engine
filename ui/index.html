🔐 Authentication Test pages behind a login ▼
How to get cookies for OTP / phone login sites:
1. Open the site in Chrome and log in normally (complete OTP on your phone)
2. Open DevTools → Application → Cookies → click your domain
3. Find access_token (or sessionid / authToken). Double-click the Value cell to select all
4. Paste it above as: access_token=<paste value here>
5. Multiple cookies: separate with ;

🔒 Your credentials are used once to authenticate and are never stored, logged, or written to disk.
